Cities RISE

A Community Empowerment Organization

Focused on Housing Inequality

Who Are We?

Cities RISE is a non-profit organization located at 601 Tully St, Syracuse NY. We originated from the Syracuse Peacemaking Project in 2020, funded through a state grant. Since 2020 we have helped hundreds of Syracuse residents improve their quality of life through achieving housing equality. Our work is far from done and we hope to partner with you soon.

What's Our Purpose?

At Cities RISE, we are working to promote housing equality in the City of Syracuse. We are doing this by partnering with community members, local charities, and the City of Syracuse to educate and empower the tenants and homeowners of Syracuse.

What can we do for you?

We offer direct assistance with the following: Connecting tenants with code enforcement Reaching out to landlords about potential code violations Scheduling a Codes Inspection Following up on open code cases and past complaints AND we make referrals to the following services and programs: Connecting with rent relief programs Learning about the Land Bank Peacemaking for neighbor disputes Reporting problematic vacant properties to the city
